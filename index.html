Midnight Chocolate Cake

Deep cocoa layers, a glossy dark drip, and a cherry that means business.

  • Prep + Bake 45 min
  • Serves 8
  • Difficulty Medium

Oven Gauge

Step 0 of 6

Pantry List

  • 200 gdark chocolate, chopped
  • 180 gunsalted butter
  • 200 gsoft light-brown sugar
  • 3large eggs, room temperature
  • 180 gplain flour
  • 40 gcocoa powder
  • 1 tspbaking powder
  • 120 mlstrong black coffee, cooled
  • 1maraschino cherry, to finish

Method

Time remaining 45:00
  1. 1

    Melt the chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over gently simmering water, stirring until glossy.

  2. 2

    Whisk the brown sugar into the warm chocolate mixture, then beat in the eggs one at a time.

  3. 3

    Sift the flour, cocoa, and baking powder over the batter and fold in gently until just combined.

  4. 4

    Stir in the cooled coffee — the batter will loosen and turn a deep, glossy brown.

  5. 5

    Pour into a lined 20 cm tin and bake at 170°C for 32–35 minutes, until a skewer comes out with a few moist crumbs.

  6. 6

    Cool completely, drip over the dark icing, and finish with a cherry before slicing.
